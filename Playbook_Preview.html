Introduction
Red Flags: Signs Your AR Process Is Broken

When accounts reach 60, 90, or 120+ days past due, the issue is no longer collections — it's a failure in process, policy, and system design.

!
No credit hold system in place
!
Continued billing on past due accounts
!

Problem No. 1
No Credit Hold System

Accounts continue receiving products or services even after balances become significantly past due — increasing financial exposure and reducing urgency for payment.

1
Define thresholds: 30 / 60 / 90 days
2
Assign ownership across AR & Sales
3
Trigger holds via ERP at 90 days
4
Require written approval for exceptions
Problem No. 3
Unapplied Cash & Credits

Accounts may appear past due when payments exist but aren't applied — causing miscommunication, wasted time, and inaccurate reporting.

"Data accuracy is just as important as collections. Decisions are only as reliable as the data behind them."

✓
Apply payments within 24–48 hours
✓
Review unapplied cash weekly
Systems & Process
Key AR Metrics to Track

% Receivables Over 90 Days

Invoice Accuracy Rate
